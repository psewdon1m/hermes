hermes call

Secure peer-to-peer video calls in a single click.

Hermes keeps your team connected with private WebRTC calls you can self-host in minutes. Share a link, join instantly, and stay in control of your data.

Leave the field empty to generate a guest identifier. Codes expire after 15 minutes, links stay valid for 24 hours.

Private by default

Hermes keeps media peer-to-peer, issues one-time access codes, and lets you host the entire stack yourself so conversations remain yours.

Open source project

How it works

  1. Click "Start call". Hermes contacts the API and issues a code plus a join link with a 24 hour token.
  2. Share the link or code. Your teammate opens /join and connects straight into the same session.
  3. Hermes manages TURN/STUN handshakes behind the scenes while the WebRTC channel stays encrypted.
